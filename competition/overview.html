We invite you to participate in the Dialogue 2021 competition on Russian News Clustering and Headline Generation. The task consists of three tracks: clustering, headline selection, and headline generation. The goal of this competition is to collect and compare different approaches to text clustering and choosing the best headline for the resulting clusters.

News documents were taken from the Telegram Data Clustering Contest. Clustering markup was collected in Yandex.Toloka with this instruction.
